# Privacy Policy for Disk Space Finder

**Last updated:** July 14, 2026

## Overview

Disk Space Finder ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

Disk Space Finder operates entirely on your local device. The app:

- Scans files and directories on your Mac's storage
- Displays disk usage information locally
- Stores scan history only on your device
- Does not transmit any data to external servers
- Does not require an internet connection to function
- Does not use analytics or tracking tools

## Data Storage

All data generated by Disk Space Finder is stored locally on your device:

- Scan results are stored in your app's local storage
- Scan history is saved to your Application Support folder
- No data is synced to iCloud or any cloud service
- No data is shared with third parties

## File Access

Disk Space Finder requires access to your files and folders to analyze disk usage. This access is:

- Used solely for displaying disk usage information
- Limited to reading file metadata (size, name, dates)
- Never used to modify, copy, or transmit your files
- Controlled by macOS permissions that you can revoke at any time

## Third-Party Services

Disk Space Finder does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy

Disk Space Finder does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository:
https://github.com/gpmarinos114/disk-space-finder

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to request, modify, or delete. All scan data stored locally can be deleted by removing the app from your device.
